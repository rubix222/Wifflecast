⚾ Batting

  • 4 balls = walk
  • 3 strikes = strikeout
  • 4 fouls = strikeout (foul-out)
  • A foul with 2 strikes adds to the foul count but does not add a strike
  • Double — a ball that lands past the double line in the air
  • Single — a ball that lands before the double line and comes to rest without being fielded
  • Tag attempt — the batter must yell "Tag" while the ball is in the air. The fielder who caught or fielded the ball throws it to home and tries to hit any part of the strike zone. The ball can bounce and roll into the zone.
  • Batters cannot lean in to get hit by a pitch — this is ruled a strike. The batter must make a reasonable attempt to get out of the way.

🏃 Base Running

  • On a hit, all runners advance the same number of bases as the hit type
  • On a walk, only runners forced advance

🧤 Fielding

  • Picking up a ball cleanly before it comes to rest is an out — this only applies to balls that first land before the double line
  • Any caught ball in the air is an out — fielders may not cross the home run line to make the catch
  • Double play — can be attempted after fielding a ball cleanly. The fielder throws the ball and must hit the strike zone in the air (no bouncing) to record the second out

⚡ Pitching

  • The pitcher's back foot must start on or behind the pitching line
  • Pitchers alternate each inning automatically
  • A pitcher may only be substituted after facing 4 or more batters in the current inning
