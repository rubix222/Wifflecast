Rules

⚾ The Count

  • 4 balls = walk (batter advances to 1B, runners advance if forced)
  • 3 strikes = strikeout — tracked as looking, swinging, or foul-out
  • 4 fouls = strikeout (foul-out)
  • A foul with 2 strikes adds to the foul count but does not add a strike

🏏 Batting

  • 1B / 2B / 3B / HR — runners advance the same number of bases as the batter
  • Ball in play (out) — tap a fielder + location to record the out
  • Error — batter reaches base; no RBI credited
  • Home run — all runners and batter score; mark where the ball left the field

📋 Innings & Game

  • Default game length: 6 innings (configurable when creating a game)
  • 3 outs end a half-inning
  • Home team bats in the bottom half; away team bats in the top half
  • Walk-off: if the home team takes the lead in the bottom of the final inning, the game ends immediately
  • Tied after regulation → extra innings until a winner is decided

🏃 Base Running

  • On a hit, all runners advance the same number of bases as the hit type
  • On a walk, only runners forced advance
  • Runs scored are credited to the batter as RBI (except on errors)
  • Runners who score are credited with an R in the stats

📊 Stats Tracked

  • Batting: AB, H, 1B, 2B, 3B, HR, BB, K, R, RBI, AVG, OBP, SLG, OPS
  • Pitching: IP, H, ER, BB, K, ERA
  • Strikeouts: broken out by looking (KL), swinging (KS), and foul-out (KF)
  • Teams: W/L/T record, runs for/against

🔧 Scoring & Admin

  • Only users with scoring privilege (granted by admin) can record plays
  • Use ↩ Undo / ↪ Redo in the scoring panel to correct mistakes
  • Tap a fielder on the field diagram to swap their position during a game
  • Admin can end a half-inning early or end a game at any time
